Photo Privacy & Storage Policy

This kiosk app is designed for physical events to capture and print souvenirs. We value your privacy and security:

  • Processing: Photos are combined with graphic templates inside the browser. No raw images are processed outside this device.
  • Digital Cloud Delivery: When you share via QR, SMS, or Email, the combined photo is uploaded to our secure content delivery network (CDN).
  • Auto-Deletion: Shared digital photos are stored with a strict TTL (Time to Live) setting and are automatically destroyed after exactly 24 hours.
  • No Resell: Your phone number or email entered during sharing is used exclusively for one-time photo receipt delivery and is not retained or sold.
